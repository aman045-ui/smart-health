Beginner Yoga - Flexibility

Improve your range of motion with simple and effective stretches for beginners.

00:00

Seated Forward Bend

Helps stretch the hamstrings and lower back. Breathe deeply and relax into the pose.

Butterfly Pose

Targets the inner thighs and hips. Sit tall and gently press knees toward the floor.

Lizard Pose

Opens the hips and stretches quads and hamstrings. Keep the spine long.

Tips for Practice:

  • Don't force flexibility; let it improve gradually.
  • Warm up before deep stretches.
  • Breathe into areas of tightness.
  • Hold each stretch for 30 seconds to 1 minute.
