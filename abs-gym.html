6-Pack Abs Workout

Ignite your core with these intense exercises designed to sculpt your abs.

00:00

Crunches

Classic core move that targets the upper abs.

Leg Raises

Works the lower abs and improves hip flexibility.

Plank

Full core activation and endurance training.

Bicycle Crunch

Engages the obliques and strengthens the full abdominal wall.

Mountain Climbers

Dynamic cardio core move that also burns fat.

Abs Workout Tips:

  • Warm up for 5–10 minutes to activate your core safely.
  • Focus on form, not just speed. Quality > Quantity.
  • Breathe out during contraction and in during release.
  • Combine with cardio & healthy diet for best results.
